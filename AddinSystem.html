Addinsystem: evaluating complex securities in realtime.

When the analytics functionality was created in the 1990's, the essential requirement was that the analytics needed to be available in realtime. Curves had to be calibrated using market inputs and securities had to be evaluated using the latest prices. This was accomplished by wrapping the functionality in Excel addins, so we referred to the analytics as the AddinSystem.
One of the core strengths of the analytics is they incorporate all of the idiosyncratic features of the many securities that Rock Ridge Advisors has encountered over the last 30 years. And if we find a new wrinkle, that wrinkle gets incorporated into the analytics in short order.

Notable Features

Structured Products - Full Intex Integration

RiskSim is fully integrated with Intex CMOSubs. You can enter deal parameters via RiskSim or use sss files.

Spreadsheets are "realtime applications".

All objects can be instantiated in spreadsheets. When combined with realtime price feeds, you can fit curves, analyze securities, and perform calculations in real time.

Carry Analysis

Devised methodology to estimate "true" carry.

Regulatory Reporting

RiskSim is designed to produce any shocks that are needed: absolute shocks, percentage shocks, etc. This makes it easy to feed a data warehouse that can be used to perform Form-PF, AIFMD, and Opera reporting.

Liqudity Analysis

Devised methodology to analyze liquidity of portfolios.

Stress Indicators

Devised methodology to determine risk of moving into a systemic environment.

Curves for any purpose.

The only limitation is the existence of fitting securities. Treasury curves. Swap curves vs. 3M fixings or 6M fixings. Currency swap curves that reflect the fx basis. OIS, EONIA, SONIA, SOFR. Default curves fit to CDS. You name it, RiskSim can produce it.

Broad Security Type Coverage

In addition to vanilla security types, RiskSim covers things such as BRL swaps, EM linkers, currency swaps. Over 100 security types. Options with various payoffs including one-touch, binary and barrier options.

Currency-adjusted Yield/Spreads

Devised methodology to adjust yields and spread to reflect rate differentials and cross-currency basis.

Default Curve Analysis

Devised flexible framework to evaluate CDS vs. cash securities for traders.
