ReconciliationTools: reporting to track the settlement and returns of a fund

What started off as some VBA routines to promote the reconciliation of security settlements grew into a full-fledged strategy P&L system that not only calculates P&L, but enables the analysis of the P&L along many dimensions.

Notable Features

Scenario Shock Analysis

Devised methodology to shock portfolio securities.

Scenario Horizon Analysis

Devised methodology to analyze contribution of hedges.

Carry Analysis

Devised methodology to estimate "true" carry.

Regulatory

Devised methodology to perform Form-PF, AIFMD, and Opera reporting.

Liqudity Analysis

Devised methodology to analyze liquidity of portfolios.

Stress Indicators

Devised methodology to determine risk of moving into a systemic environment.

Portfolio Attribution

Devised methodology to analyze attribution for total return portfolios.

Rate-Spread Decomposition

Devised methodology to break down P&L to analyse it's source.

Currency-adjusted Yield/Spreads

Devised methodology to adjust yields and spread to reflect rate differentials and cross-currency basis.

Default Curve Analysis

Devised flexible framework to evaluate CDS vs. cash securities for traders.
