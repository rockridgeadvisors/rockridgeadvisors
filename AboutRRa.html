Starting in 2009, Mr. Pennington used his vaunted RiskSim analytics suite to implement a proprietary risk and analytics system at GoldenTree Asset Management and created a macro portfolio hedging overlay to manage the risk profile of its hedge funds. Prior to that, he was a co-founder and Managing Partner at Rock Ridge Advisors, was part of the team that created Caxton Relative Value Holdings, and co-founded Convergence Asset Management. He began his career in 1986 at Salomon Brothers in the firm's renowned fixed-income proprietary trading unit. Mr. Pennington recieved his B.S. in Mathematics from Baylor University where he was the University's Highest Ranking Student and an M.B.A. from the University of Chicago where he completed all of the requirements for a Ph.D. in Finance and Statistics except for a dissertation.
